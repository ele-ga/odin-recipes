Indian Chicken Curry

Description

A tasty Indian recipe.

Gluten free and suitable for celiacs.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon curcuma
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated fresh ginger root
  • salt to taste
  • 2 chicken breast halves, cut into bit-size pieces
  • 1 can coconut milk

Steps

  1. Saute onion in olive oil until golden.
  2. Add garlic, curry powder, curcuma, ginger and salt and stir everything together for 2 minutes.
  3. Add chicken pieces and coconut milk.
  4. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes.
  5. Serve with basmati rice.
