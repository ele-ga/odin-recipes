Bean Soup

Description

A healty, easy to make bean soup.

The perfect comfort food. Gluten free and suitable for celiacs.

Ingredients

  • 3 cans borlotti beans
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon parsley
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Put all the ingredients in a pot and add 1,5 l of water.
  2. Bring to a boil. Reduce temperature and let it boil lightly for about 1 hour.
  3. Use a blender to mix together until smooth.
  4. Serve with gluten free bread or crostini.
