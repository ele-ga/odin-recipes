Falafel

Description

A delicious and healthy recipe from the Middle East.

A gluten free dish suitable for celiacs to serve in GF pita bread with a yoghurt sauce and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of chickpeas
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon parsley
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1/2 cup gluten free oats

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a food processor.
  2. When the mixture is smooth, shape it into small balls.
  3. Heat up some olive oil in a pan.
  4. Fry the falafels until they are brown on the outside.
  5. Serve with a salad or in a gluten free wrap or pita bread. Add a sauce and vegetables you have at home.
